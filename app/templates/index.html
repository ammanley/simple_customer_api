  • /customers

    Lookup all customers and their associated IDs

  • /customers/<id>

    Lookup info and orders for a specific customer ID

  • /products

    View all products and categories available

  • /products/categories

    View all products by category

  • /orders

    View all orders

  • /orders/yyyy-mm/dd/yyyy-mm-dd

    View all orders placed within a specific start/end date range

  • /orders/yyyy-mm-dd/yyyy-mm-dd/<day/week/month>

    View all orders from a specific start/end date range, and list breakdown of products sold per day/week/month
